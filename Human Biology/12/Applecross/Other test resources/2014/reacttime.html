Test Your Reaction Time

Instructions: Click on the "Start" Button. You will see the word "wait..." in the window above the button. When the window says "PUSH STOP!!!," click on the "Stop" button. You will get a 2 second penalty if you click on the "Stop" button BEFORE the window says "PUSH STOP!!!."

Click on the "New Experiment" button to start 5 new trials. The times will change as you hit the stop button.

NOTE: this test does not work if you are using an Internet EXPLORER browser. All versions of NETSCAPE will work.
